# Downtime

Time spent recovering from dungeoneering. Depending on the kind of `downtime`, you may select more or less `downtime boons`.

## Downtime Boons

Selectable when you complete `downtime`.

- Heal 1 `wound`
- Heal `hitpoints` equal to your `health`
- You re-pick 2 `arts`, `invocations`, or `blessings`
- Refresh all `cooldowns`

## Rest

Lasts 10 hours total over 4 `rounds`. The first `round` is 1 hour, `long` `activities` cannot be performed. The remaining three `rounds` are each 3 hours and any `activity` can be performed.

When complete, you gain 1 `downtime boon` and unless two of your `activities` was `sleep`, you incur the following:

- You gain `exhaustion` 1

## Vacation

Lasts one day total over 3 `rounds`. Each `round` lasts 5 hours in which any `activity` may be performed. A `chase procedure` or `combat procedure` will cancel a `vacation`. After the 15 hours of `activities`, the remainder of the day is spent on exclusively flavorful, non-adventuring tasks and ends with sleep.

When complete, you gain 3 `downtime boons` and the following:

- You regain 1 `recovery`
- You reduce `exhaustion` by 1
