# Species

Each `species` has a number of `traits` they can choose from at certain `levels`. All playable `species` are collectively referred to as "humans".

# Traits

A `trait` is a magical ability that has become naturalized to a `creature`.

## Skilled Climber

- Tags: `Boon`

When you `move` into a `tile` of wall or ceiling, the `target` is reduced by 10.

## Skilled Swimmer

- Tags: `Boon`

When you `move` into a `tile` of water, the `target` is reduced by 5.

## Clawed

- Tags: `Boon`

You have claws and may use them as a `standard` `weapon` that is always `equipped`.

## Regenerating Adrenaline

- Tags: `Boon`

You gain `regeneration` 1 whenever you begin a `combat procedure`.

## Powerful Voice

- Tags: `Boon`

Your `FT` is reduced by 1 for any `voice` `actions`.

## Camoflauge

- Tags: `Boon`

You gain the following `action`.

### Apply Camoflauge

- Tags: `Interact`
- Range: Self
- Area: Single
- Cooldown: 2

`Roll`: `talent` vs 10 (`FT` 2)

- Success: Gain `camoflauged` 1
- Critical: +1 `severity`by 1

## Simple Anatomy

- Tags: `Boon`

Whenever you gain `hitpoints`, you gain 1 additional `hitpoint`.

## Choraler

- Tags: `Boon`

You gain the following `action`:

### Chorus

- Tags: `Voice`, `Inherit`, `Slow`
- Range: 10
- Area: Single
- Cooldown: 2

You copy a `voice` `action` that was performed earlier in the `round`. The `FT` is reduced by 1.

## Burrowing Claws

- Tags: `Boon`

You gain a `burrowing` `movement type`. You have claws that act as an `improvised` `weapon`, but deal an additional 5 `damage` to `objects`.

### Burrowing

- Uses: `Prowess`
- Cooldown: 1

## Warts

- Tags: `Boon`

You start every `round` with 1 `shield` and gain 1 additional `shield` from all sources.

## Strong

- Tags: `Boon`

You deal 2 additional `damage` with `attacks` that have a `range` of 1 or 2.

## Intimidating

- Tags: `Boon`

When you inflict `fear`, your `aggregate` increases by 1d6.

## Poison Skin

- Tags: `Boon`

`Creatures` that `attack` you while adjacent to you gain `poisoned` 2.

## Powerful Leap

- Tags: `Boon`

When you `move` using your `jump` `movement type`, you can move an additional 2 `tiles`.

## Bad Climber

- Tags: `Bane`

When you `move` into a `tile` of wall or ceiling, the `target` is increased by 10.

## Bad Swimmer

- Tags: `Bane`

When you `move` into a `tile` of water, the `target` is increased by 5.

## Weak Leap

- Tags: `Bane`

When you `move` using your `jump` `movement type`, the `tiles` you can move is reduced by 2.

## Obvious Vein

- Tags: `Bane`

Your `aggregate defense` against `bleed` is always 0 (you are `weak` to `bleed`).

## Toxin Susceptibility

- Tags: `Bane`

Your `aggregate defense` against `poison` is always 0 (you are `weak` to `poison`).

## Easy Ignition

- Tags: `Bane`

Your `aggregate defense` against `burned` is always 0 (you are `weak` to `burned`).

## Wimp

- Tags: `Bane`

The `damage` of your `attacks` against adjacent targets is reduced by 1.

## Voracious

- Tags: `Bane`

Whenever you must eat, the amount is doubled.
