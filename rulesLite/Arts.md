# Arts

`Arts` are nonmagical abilities a `creature` can learn through training. A `creature` can know a number of `arts` equal to their `power` score. All `arts` have a `priority` of 0, but this can be modified via its `tag`.

# Advanced Arts

These `arts` must be unlocked explicitly, meeting any necessary prerequisites.

## Harry

- Tags: `Attack`
- Range: 1
- Area: Single

Roll: `prowess` vs `defense`

- Success: Inflict `taunted` 1d6
- Critical: +1d6 `aggregate`

## Heavy Strike

- Tags: `Attack`, `Inherit`
- Range: Self
- Area: Single

Use an `attack` and deal x2 `damage`, but your `FT` is increased by 3 for the `roll`.

## Multiattack

- Tags: `Attack`, `Inherit`
- Range: Self
- Area: Single
- Uses: `Prowess`
- Cooldown: 10

Use an `attack` twice, but your `base damage` is halved for each.

## Bash

- Tags: `Attack`
- Range: 1
- Area: Single

If using a `shield`, increase `aggregate` by 1d6

Roll: `prowess` vs `defense`

- Success: 1 `damage`; inflict `bruise` 1d6
- Critical: +1d6 `aggregate`

## Charge

- Tags: `Attack`, `Movement`
- Range: `Speed` + 1
- Area: Single

Roll: `prowess` vs `defense`

- Success: `Move` adjacent to the target; `damage` the target
- Critical: `Damage` +3

## Shank

- Tags: `Attack`
- Range: 1
- Area: Single

After using this, `cleanse` all `statuses` on the target

Roll: `prowess` vs `defense`

- Success: Deal 1 `damage` per `severity` of all `statuses` on the target
- Critical: +1 `damage`

## Disarm

- Tags: `Attack`
- Range: 1
- Area: Single
- Cooldown: 5

Roll: `prowess` vs `defense`

- Success: Deal `damage` -2
- Critical: `Creature` drops a `weapon` they have `equipped`, if possible

## Rally

- Tags: `Voice`, `Interact`
- Range: Self
- Area: Aura 4
- Cooldown: 3

Roll: `control` vs 10

- Success: Grant `shield` 2 to `creatures` of your choice
- Critical: +1 `shield`

## War Cry

- Tags: `Voice`, `Interact`
- Range: Self
- Area: Aura 4
- Cooldown: 1

Roll: `control` vs `defense`

- Success: Inflict `fear` 1d6
- Critical: +1d6 `aggregate`

## Trip

- Tags: `Attack`
- Range: 1
- Area: Single
- Cooldown: 1

Roll: `prowess` vs `defense`

- Success: Inflict `prone` 1
- Critical: `Damage` +2

## Armor Pierce

- Tags: `Attack`, `Inherit`
- Range: Self
- Area: Single
- Cooldown: 3

Use an `attack`. The `damage` ignores `shield`

## Shove

- Tags: `Attack`
- Range: 1
- Area: Single

Roll: `prowess` vs `defense`

- Success: Inflict `forced` 1d6
- Critical: +1d6 `aggregate`

## Staggaring Strike

- Tags: `Attack`
- Range: 1
- Area: Single

Roll: `prowess` vs `defense`

- Success: Deal `damage`
- Critical: Inflict `forced` 1d6

## Parry

- Tags: `Interact`, `Quick`
- Range: Self
- Area: Single
- Cooldown: 3

Gain `defense` `shield` for this `round`.

## Riposte

- Tags: `Attack`, `Slow`
- Range: 1
- Area: Single
- Cooldown: 3

Roll: `prowess` vs `defense`

- Success: Deal `damage`
- Critical: + `damage` you've taken this `round`

## Spin Attack

- Tags: `Attack`
- Range: Self
- Area: Aura 1

Roll: `control` vs `defense`

- Success: Deal `damage` -1
- Critical: +2 `damage`

## Throttle

- Tags: `Attack`, `Inherit`
- Range: Self
- Area: Single

Use an `attack`. The `damage` lowers `aggregate defense` instead of `hitpoints`.
