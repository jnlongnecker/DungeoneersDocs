# Statistic

A `statistic` (or `stat` for short) is a numerical value to describe an `entity` and provide a mechanical interaction. The `statistics` are the following:

Major

- `Power`
- `Magic`
- `Faith`
- `Talent`

Minor

- `Speed`
- `Prowess`
- `Control`
- `Defense`
- `Luck`
- `Health`

# Power

Your `power` score determines how many `arts` you have.

# Magic

Your `magic` score determines how many `invocations` you have, and gives bonus `mana`.

# Faith

Your `faith` score determines how many `blessings` you have.

# Talent

Your `talent` score determines how many `skills` you have.

# Speed

During a `round`, `creatures` with higher `speed` act first.

# Prowess

When making a `roll` to make a `targeted attack`, add your `prowess`.

# Control

When making a `roll` for an `AoE attack`, add your `control`.

# Defense

Determines `shield` given and protection against `attacks`.

# Luck

When making any `roll`, your `critical threshold` is 7 - `luck`. Also determines how many `luck points` you have.

# Health

Your `health` determines the `recoveries` and baseline number of `hitpoints` you have; you have `hitpoints` equal to 3 x `health` + `level`.

# Mana

Certain abilities require `mana` in order to function. You have `mana` equal to your `level`, plus your `magic` score.
