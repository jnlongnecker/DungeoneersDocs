# Attribute

An `attribute` is a characteristic about a `creature` used to determine effectiveness at various general tasks. An `attribute` has separate values for both the `body` and the `soul` of a `creature`. The 6 `attributes` are the following:

- `Destruction`
- `Transmutation`
- `Manipulation`
- `Translation`
- `Restoration`
- `Protection`

| Attribute     | Body                | Soul                     |
| ------------- | ------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Destruction   | Dealing Damage      | Magic damage             |
| Transmutation | Creating Equipment  | Creating potions/summons |
| Manipulation  | Trip/disarm attacks | Fear effects/convincing  |
| Translation   | Movement            | Push effects             |
| Restoration   | Healing             | Mana restore/apologies   |
| Protection    | Defending hits      | Willpower                |

The score of each `attribute` for `body` is the cumulative `body` score, while the score of each `attribute` for `soul` is the cumulative `soul` score. When an `attribute` is referred to, it will be specified as either the `body` or the `soul` score. Each are enhanced separately.

## Destruction

`Destruction` is used to enact harm upon the world. `Physical damage` is determined by the `body` score while `magic damage` is determined by the `soul` score.

## Transmutation

`Transmutation` is used to convert one thing to another. Creating physical `items` is determined by the `body` score while creating `potions` or `runes` is determined by the `soul` score.

## Manipulation

`Manipulation` is used to alter the state of things. Manipulating physical things is determined by the `body` score while manipulating minds is determined by the `soul` score.

## Transposition

`Transposition` is used to travel within `space`. Moving others is determined by the `body` score while moving the self is determined by the `soul`.

## Restoration

`Restoration` is used to recover and recuperate. Physical recovery is determined by the `body` score while mana recovery is determined by the `soul` score.

## Protection

`Protection` is used to defend against harm and change. Physical protection is determined by the `body` score while mental protection is determined by the `soul` score.

# Body

The `body` of a `creature` can only sustain so much trauma, represented as `hitpoints`. A `creature` has a number of `hitpoints` equal to their cumulative `body` score plus their `level`.

# Soul

The `soul` of a `creature` determines their capability to weild `mana`. Their `mana` is equal to their cumulative `soul` score plus their `level`.
