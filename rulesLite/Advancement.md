# Gaining Experience

Whenever you resolve an `encounter`, you gain 1 `victory`. Whenever you begin a `downtime procedure`, your `victories` turn into `experience` (1 `experience` per `victory`).

# Leveling Up

At certain `experience` thresholds, you will `level up` granting additional powers. When you do so, pick an option available to you in your current tier.

# Tier Options (1 - 5)

Pick 1 per `level up`

- ⬜⬜ `power`
- ⬜⬜ `magic`
- ⬜⬜ `talent`
- ⬜⬜ `faith`
- ⬜⬜⬜⬜ `feat`
- ⬜⬜ `speed`
- ⬜⬜ `prowess`
- ⬜⬜ `control`
- ⬜⬜ `defense`
- ⬜⬜ `luck`
- ⬜⬜⬜⬜⬜⬜⬜⬜ `health`
