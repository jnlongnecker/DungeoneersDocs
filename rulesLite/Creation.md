# Character Creation

The order you choose to do things doesn't matter, but these are the steps to make a new character:

- Pick a species
- Distribute points
- Pick features
- Pick starting equipment

# Picking a Species

You pick a single `species` from the list of `species`.

# Distribute Points

Start with all `stats` at 0 and `health` at 2. You have 4 points to spend on the following (a ⬜ indicates the number of times an option may be selected):

- ⬜⬜ `power`
- ⬜⬜ `talent`
- ⬜⬜ `magic`
- ⬜⬜ `faith`

And 6 points to spend on the following:

- ⬜⬜ `speed`
- ⬜⬜ `prowess`
- ⬜⬜ `control`
- ⬜⬜ `defense`
- ⬜⬜ `luck`
- ⬜⬜ `health`

# Picking Features

You'll need to pick features granted to you by your `power`, `talent`, `magic` and `faith`. In addition, you'll need to pick 1 `feat`.

# Picking Starting Equipment

Pick a `weapon` of choice, then spend 50 gold on `items`.
