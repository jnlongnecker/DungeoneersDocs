# Invocations

`Invocations` are magical enhancements that can be performed to bolster an `action`. A `creature` knows a number of `invocations` equal to their `magic` score.

## Extend

- Tags: `Attack`
- Cost: 1

You gain 1 `range`.

## Coat

- Tags: `Attack`
- Cost: 1

Your `damage` becomes `magic damage`.

## Lighten

- Tags: `Movement`
- Cost: 1

Increase movement by 1 `tile`.

## Empower

- Tags: `Attack`
- Cost: 2

Increase `damage` by 1.

## Expand

- Tags: `Attack`
- Cost: 2

Increase an `area` dimension by 1.

## Continuous

- Tags: `Attack`
- Cost: 3

The effect lingers. It gets re-applied on `turn` end (their turn) or when moving into the effect for the first time on a `turn`, lasting 1 `round` per application.

## Shape

- Tags: `Attack`
- Cost: 2

Change the `area` to a different shape.

## Devastate

- Tags: `Attack`
- Cost: 2

Increase `severity` by 1.

## Pierce

- Tags: `Attack`
- Cost: 2

Add 1d6 to `aggregate` `roll`.

## Carve

- Tags: `Attack`
- Cost: 2

Specify a `tile` exempt from your `area`.
