# Activity

An `activity` something that takes a while to do and cannot happen during a `combat procedure`. `Activities` with the `short` `tag` are quick enough to be done during a `social procedure` or `dungeon procedure`, `activities` with the `average` `tag` can be done in an `exploration procedure`, and `activities` with the `long` `tag` must be done during a `downtime procedure`.

# March

- Tags: `Short`, `Average`, `Long`

Move into a new `zone` or `region`.

# Channel

- Tags: `Short`

You regain all `mana`.

# Assess

- Tags: `Short`

You refresh all `cooldowns`.

# Recover

- Tags: `Short`

Heal 2 `hitpoints` per `recovery` used.

# Keep Watch

- Tags: `Short`, `Average`, `Long`

Roll: `defense` vs 10 (`FT` 1)

- Success: You and friendly `creatures` within your `zone` cannot be `ambushed`.
- Critical: You and friendly `creatures` within your `zone` gain `speedy` 1 if a `combat procedure` begins.

# Ambush

- Tags: `Short`, `Average`, `Long`

Roll: `bushcraft` vs 10

- Success: Nothing happens
- Critical: You and friendly `creatures` within your `zone` will `ambush` if a `combat procedure` beings.

# Scout

- Tags: `Short`, `Average`, `Long`

You scout an adjacent `zone` or `region` of your choice in order to learn the following:

- How dangerous is it?
- How difficult is it to move through?
- Are there `creatures`?
- Is it a dead end?
- Follow-up questions on one of the above
- Other question as approved by the GM

Roll: `speed` vs 15 (`FT` 1)

- Success: You learn 1 thing of your choice
- Critical: You learn a different thing of your choice.

# Toss Over

- Tags: `Short`, `Average`, `Long`

You look for something in particular or describe your method of search.

Roll: `searching` vs 10

- Success: You find 1 thing that is findable and relevant
- Elevate: You find 1 additional thing that is findable and relevant

# Navigate

- Tags: `Short`, `Average`, `Long`

Roll: `navigation` vs 10

- Success: You learn which `region` gets you closest to your destination
- Critical: You learn the optimal next `region` to take

# Use Skill

- Tags: `Short`, `Average`, `Long`

Utilize a relevant `skill`. The base `difficulty` for any `roll` is 10.

# Advance Project

- Tags: `Long`

Make a roll for a `total` towards one of the `projects` you are working on.

# Retrain

- Tags: `Long`

Roll for a `total` using a `major stat`. You may re-pick that many `arts`, `invocations`, or `blessings` you are benefitting from.

# Sleep

- Tags: `Long`

You gain `sleep` for this `round` and reduce `exhaustion` by 1.

# Leisure & Chores

- Tags: `Long`

You perform some activity of leasure or preparation for adventure, such as shopping.
