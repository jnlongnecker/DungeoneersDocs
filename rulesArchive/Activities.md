# Activity

An `activity` something that takes a while to do and cannot happen during a `combat procedure`.

# March

- Cost: 2 `AP`

Move into a new `zone` or `region` carefully.

# Rush

- Cost: 1 `AP`

Move into a new `zone` or `region` quickly. You will trigger unidentified traps in your path.

# Toss Over

- Cost: 1 `AP`

You look for something in particular or describe your method of quick search.

Roll: `searching` vs 15

- Success: You find 1 thing that is findable and relevant
- Elevate: You find 1 additional thing that is findable and relevant

# Comb

- Cost: 2 `AP`

You look for something in particular or describe your method of thorough search.

Roll: `searching` vs 10

- Success: You find 1 thing that is findable and relevant
- Elevate: You find 1 additional thing that is findable and relevant

# Scout

- Cost: 2 `AP`

You scout an adjacent `zone` or `region` of your choice in order to learn the following:

- How dangerous is it?
- How difficult is it to move through?
- Are there `creatures`?
- Is it a dead end?
- Follow-up questions on one of the above
- Other question as approved by the GM

Roll: `speed` vs 15 (`FT` 1)

- Success: You learn 1 thing of your choice
- Critical: You learn a different thing of your choice.

# Navigate

- Cost: 1 `AP`

Roll: `navigation` vs 15

- Success: You learn which `region` gets you closest to your destination
- Critical: You learn the optimal next `region` to take

# Quick Skill

- Cost: 1 `AP`

Utilize a relevant `skill` quickly. The base `difficulty` for any `roll` is 15.

# Use Skill

- Cost: 2 `AP`

Utilize a relevant `skill` at a normal pace. The base `difficulty` for any `roll` is 10.
