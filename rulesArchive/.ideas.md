Stats: Faith, Magic, Power, Talent

- Faith: Gives Blessings
- Magic: Gives Invocations + extra Mana
- Power: Gives Arts
- Talent: Gives Skills

# Invocations

Use on top of an action to accentuate it. Costs mana

- Extend range by 1 tile
- Change damage type
- Give 1 extra tile of movement
- Add element
- Extend AOE

# Arts

Nonmagical base moves

Can add mana on top

# Blessings

Depends on God

# Skills

Grants access to skilled work. Can add talent to rolls relevant to skill

---

Each should grant in and out of combat benefits

Mana = level + Magic

- Faith | Magic -> Magical damage
- Power | Talent -> Physical damage
