# Inventory

Each creature has 15 inventory slots to store items. Some items stack; this is specified. For unlisted items, for an item to stack multiple of it must be able to fit in a single hand.

# Weapons

Weapons are classified into categories: improvised, light, standard, control, and heavy.

## Improvised

The default weapon category. Provides no special bonuses, but requires no hands to use.

## Light

Gives +1 acting speed. Requires 1 hand to use.

## Standard

Gives -1 miss chance. Requires 1 hand to use.

## Control

Gives +1 range. Requires 2 hands to use.

## Heavy

Gives +1 damage. Requires 2 hands to use.
