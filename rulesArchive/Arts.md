# Arts

`Arts` are nonmagical abilities a `creature` can learn through training. A `creature` can know a number of `arts` equal to their `power` score. `Common arts` do not count towards this total. All `arts` have a `priority` of 0, but this can be modified via its `tag`.

# Common Arts

`Common arts` are known by all `creatures` and do not count towards known `arts` limits.

## Stride

- Tags: `Movement`, `Slow`
- Range: Self
- Area: Single
- Cost: 1 `AP`

You move up to your `speed`

## Speak

- Tags: `Voice`
- Range: Self
- Area: Single
- Cost: 1 `AP`

You speak to your surroundings.

## Defend

- Tags: `Interact`, `Quick`, `Reaction`
- Range: Self
- Area: Single
- Cost: 1 `AP`

You gain `shield`.

## Interact

- Tags: `Interact`
- Range: 1
- Area: Single
- Cost: 1 `AP`

You perform some interaction with something in range. This can be one of many things; e.g. opening a door, pulling a lever, using an `item` in your `inventory`, retrieving an `item` from a container, picking up an `object`, or accepting/handing off something to someone else.

## Help

- Tags: `Interact`, `Quick`
- Range: 2
- Area: Single
- Cost: 1 `AP`

You donate 1 `result pool` die to the target as they are making a `roll`.

## Channel

- Tags: `Interact`
- Range: 1
- Area: Single
- Cost: 1 `AP`

Roll: major `stat` vs remaining `mana`

- Success: Gain 2 `mana`
- Critical: Gain 3 `mana`

## Flee/Pursue

- Tags: `Movement`, `Quick`
- Range: Self
- Area: Single
- Cost: 1 `AP`

You attempt to flee from `combat`, or pursue after another `creature` who has fled. This `action` causes a `chase procedure` to begin if it results in a flee-er and a pursuer.

If you flee and are not pursued, you may not partake in the `combat procedure` until it is over.

# Advanced Arts

These `arts` must be unlocked explicitly, meeting any necessary prerequisites.

## Harry

- Tags: `Attack`
- Range: 1
- Area: Single
- Cost: 1 `AP`

Roll: `defense` (major `stat`)

- Success: Inflict `taunted` (1d6 `aggregate`, `severity` 1)
- Elevation: Increase `severity` by 1
