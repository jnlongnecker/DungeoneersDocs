# Core

| Attribute     | Body                | Soul                     |
| ------------- | ------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Destruction   | Physical damage     | Magic damage             |
| Transmutation | Creating Equipment  | Creating potions/summons |
| Manipulation  | Trip/disarm attacks | Fear effects/convincing  |
| Transposition | Movement            | Combat Speed             |
| Restoration   | Healing             | Mana restore/apologies   |
| Protection    | Defending hits      | Willpower                |

Each round, each creature can activate actions granted by an attribute. Only 1 action per group can be activated.

- Destruction & Restoration
- Protection & Transposition
- Manipulation & Transmutation

Destruction

Roll to hit, then deal physical (body) or magical damage (soul)

Transmutation

Roll to hit, then apply physical status damage (body) or apply soul status damage (soul)

Manipulation

Use an item

Transposition

Move (body) or increase range (soul)

Restoration

Restore stamina (body) or mana (soul)

Protection

Grant a damage shield (body) or status damage shield (soul)

---

Roll 3d6, allocate results accordingly

---

Creatures have:

- health = body total + soul total
- mana = soul total
- stamina = body total
- status defense = body protection score + soul protection score

---

Activating a capability requires 1 stamina for the body version or 1 mana for the soul version, plus the number of dice you apply. If you have 0 or fewer stamina or mana, you take double damage, cannot move and cannot use dice
