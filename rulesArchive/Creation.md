# Character Creation

The order you choose to do things doesn't matter, but these are the steps to make a new character:

- Pick a species
- Distribute points
- Pick features

# Picking a Species

You pick a single `species` from the list of `species`.

# Distribute Points

Start with all major `stats` at 0 and all other `stats` at 3. You have 9 points to spend on the following (a ⬜ indicates the number of times an option may be selected):

- ⬜⬜ `power`
- ⬜⬜ `talent`
- ⬜⬜ `magic`
- ⬜⬜ `faith`
- ⬜⬜⬜ `speed`
- ⬜⬜⬜ `prowess`
- ⬜⬜⬜ `control`
- ⬜⬜⬜ `defense`
- ⬜⬜⬜ `luck`
- ⬜⬜⬜ `health`

# Picking Features

You'll need to pick features granted to you by your `power`, `talent`, `magic` and `faith`. In addition, you'll need to pick 1 `feat`.
