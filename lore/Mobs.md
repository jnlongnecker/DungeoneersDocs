# Mobs

A mob (or more correctly, MOB) is an acronym that stands for Mana Originated Being. Mobs are semi-living beings that manifest from the mana of elemental entities. For example, dark-elemental mobs originated from the mana of toxins, decay, and darkness, while water-elemental mobs originate from the mana of liquids.

Mobs are volatile by their very nature and seek to destroy potent mana sources that do not match their own. This results in an environment that becomes increasingly homogenous and struggles to sustain itself and eventually collapse. These environments quickly spiral into potent breeding grounds of dark mana. These dead environments are rare cases of self-sustaining, mana rich environments which threaten to spread without limit.

Mobs do not produce mana, instead they manipulate the mana that fuels their existence. Should a mob be destroyed, the mana that fuels it will gradually dissipate into free mana. Useful parts to a mob must be constantly harvested as they deteriorate rapidly once extracted. Each mob has a core that it forms around, a collection of mana that has been so hyper-condensed it has crystallized.

Once spawned, mobs do not tend to gain or lose power. Instead, they will utilize their mana, releasing it back into the environment to mix with the existing free mana produced by the mana sources. As mobs tend to destroy conflicting mana sources and nurse aligned sources, this process results in more and more mana and the creation of more powerful mobs. The presence of extremely powerful mobs is evidence of a long-neglected environment.
